// HOW IT WORKS

Crumpled paper in.
Household autopilot out.

01

Snap it

JPG, PNG, HEIC, PDF. Phone, Telegram bot, or drag-and-drop.

02

Parse it

Four OCR models in a fallback chain. Cheapest one that clears 95% confidence wins.

03

Own it

Line items land in SQLite on your disk. Inventory, budgets, and price history update themselves.

The cloud is just someone else's computer.

Every receipt is a record of how you live. LocalOCR keeps that record where it belongs — on hardware you control. Open source, MIT licensed, audit every line.

// WHAT IT RUNS

One scan feeds everything.

Inventory autopilot

Pantry counts update on every scan. Low-stock alerts before you run out.

Shopping list

Groups by store, swipe to mark bought, suggests where it's cheapest.

Bills & forecasting

Recurring obligations tracked with cadence-aware due-date forecasts.

Price history

Store wars, settled with data. See what every item cost, everywhere, over time.

Household ledger

Who paid, who owes, who actually does the shopping. Contribution scoring built in.

Telegram intake

Snap a receipt in chat; it's parsed before you pocket the phone.

Home Assistant

MQTT events for your automations. Out of milk → light turns red. Your call.

QR device pairing

Trusted devices with scoped permissions. No accounts on anyone's server.
