Questions

Asked, answered.

Does it work fully offline?

Yes. Point the pipeline at Ollama LLaVA and OCR runs entirely on your LAN. No internet required, no receipt ever leaves the building.

What hardware do I need?

Anything that runs Docker — ARM64 or x86_64. A Raspberry Pi, an old laptop, a NAS in the closet. If it boots, it scans.

Is my data really mine?

It's a SQLite file on your disk. Backups are local tarballs. There is no LocalOCR server to send anything to — that's the whole point.

What does OCR actually cost?

Your choice. Gemini Flash parses a receipt for fractions of a cent, Ollama is free and local. The chain always picks the cheapest model that clears 95% confidence.
