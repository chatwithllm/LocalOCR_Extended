How it works

Three steps. No drawer of shame.

From crumpled paper to queryable data in under two seconds.

Drop it.

Phone photo, scanner PDF, HEIC, email forward. If it looks like a receipt, it goes in the hopper.

Read it.

Three vision models vote. Fields get confidence scores. Low-confidence flags route to a 30-second review.

Own it.

Clean rows in SQLite. Queryable, exportable, your data. Export to CSV, JSON, or your accounting tool of choice.

Plaid-connected · 12,000+ banks & cards

The other half of the ledger.

Not every expense comes with a paper receipt. Connect your bank and card accounts via Plaid — LocalOCR pulls every transaction and reconciles it against scanned receipts automatically.

  • Auto-match — receipt ←→ transaction by vendor, amount, date
  • Auto-fill — subscriptions, ACH, recurring charges with no paper trail
  • Flag gaps — “you swiped at Walgreens, no receipt” (you can ignore it)
  • Tokens only — Plaid holds your credentials; LocalOCR only sees the transactions

Provider mix

Three vision models. One accuracy number that keeps going up.

Each receipt runs against the fastest provider first. If confidence falls below 0.70, it retries against a second. If that fails, the third. You bring the keys, LocalOCR orchestrates.

Gemini 2.5 Flash
PRIMARY · 68% of traffic
Fastest. Cheapest. Wins most legible receipts.
Claude Vision
FALLBACK · 24% of traffic
Best at faded, crumpled, or rotated images.
OpenAI Vision
BACKUP · 8% of traffic
Last resort for odd layouts and foreign scripts.
